Youth Church Members Organize Pad-Making Drive to Support Those in Need

The youth members of our local church are stepping up to make a difference with an inspiring initiative, a Pad-Making Event designed to provide reusable, cloth-based sanitary pads for those in need.

This compassionate project aims to address period poverty by creating sustainable solutions and distributing these handmade pads to women and girls who lack access to essential hygiene products.

If you or someone you know could benefit from this initiative, we invite you to come and collect these pads.

Additionally, we welcome donations to support the purchase of materials and ensure this initiative reaches as many lives as possible.
